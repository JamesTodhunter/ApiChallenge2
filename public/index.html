The images you can search are pictures taken on sol 1000 which is the equilvant of Earth date of 05-30-2015. Sol are Mars version of earth days. The rover named Curiosity took all the pictures you can view by either typing 0 through 856, when you search you will see Fhaz, navcam or mast appear above the image, Fhaz stands for front hazard avoidance camera, mast is Curiosity' s mast camera, and navcam is Curiosity's navigation camera. These are three out of 17 cameras Curiosity has. Curiosity has been on planet Mars for 3249 sols or 3338 Earth days or 9 years, 51 days as of August 5th 2021.
